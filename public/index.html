How It Works

Provide a clear image of your face. Our AI analyzes your features to extract skin tone and face shape data.

Step 3: AI-Powered Analysis

Our system evaluates your skin tone to suggest a range of flattering colors and identifies your face shape for hairstyle recommendations.

Step 4: Tailored Recommendations

Receive a comprehensive report with curated most suitable color and hairstyle that suit your unique attributes.
